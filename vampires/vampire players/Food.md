# List of consumable things

**Items**
- Golden apples
- Bottle of blood
- Vampire blood
- Vampire Lord blood
- Holy water


**Entity blood**
- Player
- Villager
- Sheep
- Cow
- Camel
- Pig
- Chicken
- Horse
- Mule
- Axolotl
- Parrot
- Donkey
- Dolphin

**Exceptions**
- Player entity:
  You can not drink from a player entity if they are a vampire or they have something in their chestplate and helmet slots.
